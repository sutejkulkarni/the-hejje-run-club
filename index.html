üîÅ 3-Phase Training Cycle

We train with purpose, not pressure. Each program follows a 3-phase cycle:

  1. Base Building: Endurance, aerobic strength, injury resistance
  2. Intensification: Speed, intervals, fartlek, long runs
  3. Tapering: Recovery, freshness, race readiness

Every cycle ends with a fun workout (props, ladders, cones) to celebrate progress.
